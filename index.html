Rob Griffin: Profile
I am a statistical programmer at AstraZeneca working on research and development related to respiratory, inflammatory and autoimmune diseases. Prior to joining AstraZeneca in 2020, I performed research in evolutionary quantitative genetics. I received my PhD in Evolutionary Genetics from Uppsala University in 2015, and held positions as a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Turku, Uppsala University and the University of Queensland. I work with and practise a number of programming languages, mostly using R and SAS for statistical analysis, data processing and data visualisation. I also use HTML, CSS and JavaScript for front-end web development, and XML and Kotlin/Java for Android app development, and have dabbled with Python, Perl and LaTeX.
